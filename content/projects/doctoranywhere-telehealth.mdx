## Overview

DoctorAnywhere is a telehealth platform operating across Southeast Asia. I led design on the patient-side mobile app and the clinician web interface.

## Scope

- Patient: appointment booking, video consultation, prescription and medication delivery
- Clinician: consultation queue, patient history, e-prescription workflow
- Cross-cutting: accessibility audit, design system foundation

## Outcome

Shipped to production in SG, MY, TH. Clinician onboarding time reduced by 35%.
