## Overview

Profita is LH Bank's mutual fund investment platform, built at Robowealth. The design challenge was making complex financial products approachable for first-time investors while satisfying SEC compliance requirements.

## The Constraint

Bi-weekly Legal and Compliance reviews meant every design decision had a regulatory dimension. The designs that survived were the ones where the compliance case was already visible in the prototype.

## Outcome

Launched 2020. Won Best App for Customer Experience at the Retail Banker International Asia Trailblazer Awards (2023). First-time investor completion rate exceeded internal benchmark.
