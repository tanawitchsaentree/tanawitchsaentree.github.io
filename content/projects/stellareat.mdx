## Overview

Stellareat uses AI to generate personalised recipe recommendations based on ingredient inventory, dietary constraints, and learned taste preferences.

## The Challenge

Recommendation surfaces fail when users don't trust them. The design problem wasn't the algorithm — it was making the reasoning legible so users would engage with suggestions rather than ignore them.

## What I Did

- Designed the onboarding preference model (dietary + taste axes)
- Built the recipe card surface with progressive disclosure for ingredient substitutions
- Defined the feedback loop — how explicit thumbs and implicit behaviour fed back into recommendations
