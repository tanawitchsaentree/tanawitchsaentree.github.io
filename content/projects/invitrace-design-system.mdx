## Overview

Invitrace needed a design system that could scale across 6 product teams without creating a centralised bottleneck.

## The Model Choice

Two options: gatekeeping (one team controls everything) or federated contribution (teams own their domains, shared governance). We picked federated and built the governance to make it safe.

## What I Built

- Token architecture (semantic + primitive layers)
- Component contribution process
- Accessibility baseline (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Documentation that engineers actually read
