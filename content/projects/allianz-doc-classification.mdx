## Overview

Allianz processes thousands of incoming documents every day — claims, invoices, legal notices, correspondence. IDAS is the internal system that uses AI to classify each one and route it to the right team.

This tool is the layer that controls how that AI thinks.

## The Problem

Before IDAS, document triage was manual. Skilled claims handlers spent hours every day doing mechanical routing — reading documents, categorizing them, forwarding to the right queue. Not analysis. Not decision-making. Just sorting.

When the AI was introduced, the problem didn't disappear — it shape-shifted. The AI was fast. But when it was wrong, no one knew until something downstream broke. And when it was right, handlers still double-checked everything, because there was no way to know which result to trust.

**The volume was there. The confidence wasn't.**

## Key Insight

Users don't fear AI being wrong. They fear being wrong without knowing it.

The goal wasn't to make the AI more accurate. It was to make the AI's uncertainty legible — so configurators could act on it, not just be surprised by it.

## What Changed

Configurators who had avoided touching prompts for weeks started iterating daily — not because anyone asked them to, but because a safe place to try now existed.
