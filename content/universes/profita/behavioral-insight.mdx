## The Problem

Profita launched as a wealth management app for LH Bank's retail customers.
Early testing revealed something the dashboard didn't predict: users opened
the app, looked at the numbers, and closed it. Not because the data was
wrong — but because the data was overwhelming.

## Key Insight

We coded user interviews looking for confusion patterns. The pattern that
emerged wasn't confusion. It was anxiety. Users felt judged by their own
portfolios. Progress bars made them feel behind. Goal completion percentages
reminded them how far they had to go.

## What Changed

We rewrote the app's tone from "performance" to "guidance." Instead of
"Your goal is 23% complete," we tested "You've started. Here's what's next."
Engagement in trial phase increased.
