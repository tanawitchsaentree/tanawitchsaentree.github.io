## The Conflict

Bi-weekly reviews became battlegrounds. Business wanted aggressive
calls-to-action: "Invest more!" "Don't miss this opportunity!" Legal wanted
disclaimers everywhere — paragraphs of risk warnings before every action.
Both were right. Neither was usable.

## The Approach

I introduced logic-based reviews. Instead of "I think this works," proposals
came with: the user behavior we expected, the regulation we were addressing,
the risk we were carrying, the alternative we ruled out. Conversations
shifted from preferences to trade-offs.

## What Changed

Compliance approved a layered disclaimer pattern. Business got their CTAs
but with intentional friction — confirm screens for high-impact actions.
