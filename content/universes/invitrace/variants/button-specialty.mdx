description: "Specialty clinics serve narrow workflows. I locked the button pattern almost completely — one density, one label position, one color. The team asked for more flexibility, but more flexibility meant more places for inconsistency to live. A focused system serving a focused workflow doesn't need optionality."

  - "icon usage (none — text only)"

  - "focus ring treatment and offset"
  - "disabled state contrast ratio"
  - "keyboard activation (Enter + Space)"
  - "density (regular only — no compact)"
reasoning: "Specialty teams often have a single clinic manager who configures the system. One pattern is easier to audit than three. Flexibility here was a governance risk, not a product feature."
