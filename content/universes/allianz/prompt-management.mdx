## The Problem

Business configurators needed to tune how the AI classified documents — but every change required an engineering ticket, a deployment window, and days of waiting.

When the AI behaved unexpectedly, the only way to debug it was to ask an engineer what the prompt said.

## Key Insight

A prompt is a policy document. It needs a draft/review/publish workflow, not a code deployment pipeline.

Giving configurators direct access — with a test harness built in — collapses the feedback loop from days to minutes.

## What Changed

Prompt iteration cycles went from weekly (blocked by engineering queue) to daily. Business configurators could now own the AI's behavior end-to-end.
